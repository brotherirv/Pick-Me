About the Creator

Irvin always loved playing instruments with musicians. Since the age of 12, he had started collaborating with other musicians. After a good few years, he realised that it was difficult to find msicians. He always needed a bass player, or a singer, or even a drummer. It was hard to find other musicians to collaborate with. It has always been a problem for him, and he realised that other musicians have the same problem. Which is why he created pick me, the easiest way to communicate with other musicians.
